About 自己紹介

I am a second-year Ph.D. student at the Graduate School of Mathematics, Nagoya University. My research interests include category theory, homological algebra, and representation theory of algebras. In particular, I am interested in DG-algebras, DG-categories, and extriangulated categories.

名古屋大学多元数理科学研究科の博士後期課程2年です。 圏論，ホモロジー代数，代数の表現論に関心があり，特に DG-algebra，DG-categories，Extriangulated Categories に興味があります。
